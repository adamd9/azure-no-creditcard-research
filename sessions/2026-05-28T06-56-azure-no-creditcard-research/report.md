**Source:** https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/pricing/purchase-options/azure-account

**Credit Card Required?** ⚠️ YES — but only for identity verification. Card is NOT charged at signup.

> "We use the phone number and credit card or debit card for identity verification to validate that account holders are real people and not bots. We don't charge your credit card... but you may see a one-dollar (or equivalent) verification hold... which will be reversed upon verification."

**Eligibility:** New Azure customers only  
**Benefits:**
- $200 credit to use in first 30 days
- 20+ popular services free for 12 months (new customers)
- 65+ always-free services
- Spending protection: card won't be charged while within free limits

**Limitations:**
- Credit card required at signup (even though it won't be charged)
- Only for new Azure customers
- $200 credit expires after 30 days
- Only credit cards accepted in Hong Kong and Brazil (not debit)

**Offer Number:** MS-AZR-0044P

## KEY NOTES & EXCEPTIONS

### Regional Variations
- **Hong Kong & Brazil:** Only credit cards accepted (no debit cards) even for the standard free account
- **India:** UPI and Netbanking supported as payment methods
- **Germany & Netherlands & Austria:** SEPA bank transfer supported as payment method
- Sovereign clouds (Azure China, Azure US Government, Azure Germany): Not eligible for standard free offers

### What "No Credit Card" Actually Means
- For **Azure for Students**, **Students Starter**, **Visual Studio**, **Azure Pass**, **Nonprofits**, **Startups (Founders Hub)**: Literally no credit card is collected or required at signup
- For **Azure Free Account**: A credit card IS collected for identity verification, but a $1 authorization hold is immediately reversed; you won't be charged

### Prepaid / Virtual Cards
- Prepaid cards (Visa gift cards, etc.) are **NOT accepted** as valid payment instruments for any Azure subscription
- This applies globally

### The Microsoft Learn Sandbox Shutdown
- As of October 2025, free Azure sandbox environments for training are **no longer available**
- Azure for Students or the Azure Free Account are now recommended for learners

### Azure Pass Promo Codes
- Not publicly distributed; must be obtained by attending official Microsoft training
- One code per Microsoft Account
- Use a **brand-new Microsoft Account** when redeeming (accounts that have had prior Azure subscriptions may be ineligible)
